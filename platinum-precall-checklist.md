## 💡 Tips for Preparation

1. **Start Early**: Begin gathering information at least one week before your call. This gives you time to locate documents and reach out to guardians if needed.

2. **Organize by Pet**: If you have multiple pets, create a separate section for each one to keep information clear and organized.

3. **Ask Your Guardian**: Contact your chosen guardian and ask them to gather information about their current pet care setup, lifestyle, and any questions they have.

4. **Contact Your Vet**: Reach out to your veterinarian's office to request copies of medical records and vaccination history.

5. **Review Your Estate Documents**: If you have a will or trust, review it before the call to discuss any updates needed.

6. **Make Notes**: Jot down any questions or concerns you have about your pet's care or your planning process.

7. **Be Honest**: There are no "wrong" answers. Share your true situation, concerns, and wishes so we can create a plan that truly works for you and your pet.
